# NFC Attender - User Guide

Welcome to NFC Attender! This guide will help you understand how to use the attendance tracking system.



## **CHECK THAT YOU HAVE A REGISTERED CARD WITH LILY**

## Daily Check-In Process

**Morning Check-In**

- **Tap your NFC card** on the reader when you arrive
- If you check in **before 10:00 AM**, you'll be marked as **Present** ✅
- If you check in **at 10:00 AM or later**, you'll be marked as **Late** ⚠️

**Lunch Break**

1. **Going to lunch**: Tap your NFC card after 1:00 PM to check out for lunch
2. **Returning from lunch**: Tap your NFC card again to check back in
    - Return **before 2:00 PM** = On time for lunch ✅
    - Return **after 2:00 PM** = Late from lunch ⚠️

**End of Day Check-Out**

- Tap your NFC card **after 4:59 PM** to check out for the day

## Understanding Your Status

🟢 **Present** — Checked in on time

🟡 **Late** — Checked in after the deadline

🔴 **Absent** — Did not check in

⚪ **No Status** — Not yet checked in today

## Dashboard Overview

**Your Card**

On the main dashboard, you can see your attendance card showing:

- Your **name** and **email**
- Today's **check-in time**
- Your current **status** (Present/Late)
- **Lunch times** (if applicable)
- **Check-out time** (end of day)

**Status Colors**

- **Green border** = Present (on time)
- **Yellow border** = Late
- **Gray** = Not checked in yet

## Viewing Your History

1. Click the **📊 History** button on the dashboard
2. Select a **date** to view attendance for that day
3. You can search for specific learners by name or email
4. View detailed information including:
    - Check-in time
    - Lunch out/in times
    - Check-out time
    - Status for the day

## Tips for Success

1. **Arrive early** — Check in before 10:00 AM to be marked as Present
2. **Don't forget lunch** — Tap when leaving AND returning from lunch
3. **Check out at the end** — Tap after 4:59 PM before leaving
4. **Keep your NFC card safe** — You need it for all check-ins

## Troubleshooting

**"Card not recognized"**

- Make sure you're using the correct NFC card
- Hold the card steady on the reader for 1-2 seconds
- Contact an administrator if the problem persists

**"Already checked in"**

- You can only check in once per time slot
- If you need to correct your attendance, ask an administrator

**Missing from the system**

- If you're a new learner, an administrator needs to add you first
- Make sure your NFC card has been registered

## Need Help?

Contact your administrator if you:

- Lost your NFC card
- Need to correct attendance records
- Have questions about the system
